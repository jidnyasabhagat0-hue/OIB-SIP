About Him

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was one of India’s most respected scientists and the 11th President of India. He played a leading role in India's missile and space programs, earning the title “Missile Man of India”.

What truly sets him apart wasn’t power or position — it was humility. He connected with students, believed in education, and dedicated his life to inspiring young minds to dream big.

Even as President, he chose classrooms over luxury. That’s rare. That’s leadership.
